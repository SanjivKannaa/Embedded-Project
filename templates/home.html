Gas Leakage Detection System







Welcome to our gas leakage detection system. In today's world, where safety and environmental concerns are paramount, our system provides an essential layer of protection against potential hazards.

Our system utilizes a range of components including Raspberry Pi, gas sensors (such as MQ2), fire sensors, and GSM technology, all interconnected to detect and alert about gas leakage promptly.

Gas leaks can lead to serious health risks, environmental pollution, and even explosions. Hence, early detection and immediate action are crucial in preventing accidents and ensuring safety.
